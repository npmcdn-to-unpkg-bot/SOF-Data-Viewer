Our Guidance

We used the Virginia Standards of Learning (SOL) and the United States Common Core as our initial survey of what goals and requirements students and teachers are striving to achieve. We reviewed each grade level's components, in particular the components that focus around the core ideas of teaching fractions through alternative, embodied, mediated, and musical methods.

    • 3.NF.A.1
      • Develop Understanding of fractions as Numbers

      • Explain equivalence of fractions and compare fractions by reasoning about their size

        2. b) recognize and generate simple equivalent fractions
        3. c)recognize whole numbers as fractions
        4. d) Compare 2 fractions with the same numerator or denominator with <, >, =

    • 4.NF.A.1
      • Extend Understanding of Fraction Equivalence and Ordering

      • Build Fractions from Unit Fraction
