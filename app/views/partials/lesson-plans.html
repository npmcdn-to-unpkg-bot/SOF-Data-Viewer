We approach our daily lesson plans and activities similar guided by XXX and use the 'DO', 'ASK', and 'TAKEAWAY' to help guide us in instructing the students.

Each Lesson plan has the following:

  • A Name to help reference the activity
  • A materials list to help plan
  • Setup guidance for each activity
  • Notes for assisting Facilitators or helpers
  • A location to help remind the researchers which location is receiving the lesson plans
  • The day, activity number for that day, and an estimation of how long the activity will take.
  • One (or more) task that the teacher and/or student(s) will perform.
  • Questions that coincide with each 'DO' activity.
  • A guiding Takeaway message to help the teachers, researchers, and facilitators guide their teaching decisions when working with the student(s).
  • A list of the Virginia Standards Of Learning (SoL) numbers and brief description.
Please note, that research and class instruction is not perfect.
